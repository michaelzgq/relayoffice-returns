# Returns Landing Page Copy

最后更新时间：2026-04-06 America/Los_Angeles

## Goal

这是一版极简一页式 landing page 文案。

目标不是讲全产品，而是让目标客户在 `30-60` 秒内明白：

1. 你解决的不是 shopper portal
2. 你解决的是仓库侧 exception handling
3. 你适合多品牌 3PL
4. 下一步是约一个 `20` 分钟 call 或看 `3` 分钟 demo

## Section 1: The Problem

### Section Title

`The hard part starts after the item reaches the warehouse`

### Body Copy

`Most 3PLs already have some way to handle customer-facing returns. The messy part starts when the returned unit is physically back in the warehouse.`

`Each client brand wants different grading rules, different evidence, and different refund thresholds. Most teams still run that workflow from SOP docs, spreadsheets, Slack messages, and tribal knowledge.`

### Pain Bullets

- `Brand A wants serial + packaging proof, Brand B does not`
- `Inspectors are expected to remember too many client-specific rules`
- `High-risk cases move forward without complete evidence`
- `Ops leaders find backlog or SLA risk too late`
- `Wrong item, empty box, and damage disputes are hard to defend`

## Section 2: The Product

### Section Title

`A lightweight control layer for warehouse-side return exceptions`

### Body Copy

`We do not replace your WMS or shopper-facing returns portal. We standardize what happens after the return arrives: how the unit is judged, what proof must be captured, what the warehouse should do next, and whether refund should move forward yet.`

### Feature Block 1

**Title**  
`Client Playbooks`

**Body**  
`Give each brand its own inspection logic: allowed conditions, evidence requirements, recommended warehouse actions, and refund defaults.`

### Feature Block 2

**Title**  
`Inspect From a Phone Browser`

**Body**  
`Warehouse teams can inspect a return, upload photos, and follow the correct client playbook without installing another app.`

### Feature Block 3

**Title**  
`Refund Queue`

**Body**  
`Ops can hold, review, or release based on evidence completeness instead of guesswork.`

### Feature Block 4

**Title**  
`Case Timeline + Evidence Pack`

**Body**  
`Every exception case has a timeline and export-ready evidence record for disputes, audits, and client communication.`

## Section 3: Best Fit

### Section Title

`Best fit for multi-brand 3PLs`

### Body Copy

`This product is designed for 3PLs managing returns across multiple DTC brands, especially when each client has different warehouse-side rules.`

### Good Fit Bullets

- `You serve multiple ecommerce brands from one warehouse network`
- `Return rules vary by client`
- `Inspectors still rely on SOP docs, sheets, or training`
- `You deal with wrong item, empty box, damage, or missing parts cases`
- `You want a lighter starting point than a full reverse-logistics rollout`

### Not A Fit Bullets

- `You only need a shopper-facing returns portal`
- `You are replacing your full WMS`
- `You already have a mature warehouse exception-control system and are happy with it`

## Usage Notes

- 首屏不要提太多竞品名字
- 首屏不要说 “AI”
- 首屏不要说 “all-in-one”
- 首屏不要讲 full returns management
- 首屏只卖一个认知：`warehouse-side exception control`
