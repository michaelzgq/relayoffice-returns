# Dossentry PDF Outreach Follow-Ups

Last updated: 2026-04-13 America/Los_Angeles

## Goal

This file turns the new Dossentry customer PDFs into usable sales follow-up assets.

The rule is simple:

- do **not** lead with the PDF
- lead with `compare page` or landing page
- send the PDF only after interest, reply, or a real conversation

Why:

- first touch should validate message, not dump collateral
- the compare page is a lower-friction click than an attachment
- the PDF is strongest as a trust-building artifact after the buyer already cares

## Which PDF To Send

Send the English PDF when:

- the buyer operates in English
- the contact asks for more info
- the contact agrees to a follow-up after clicking compare or landing

Send the Chinese PDF when:

- the buyer or operator is Chinese-speaking
- you are doing WeChat, Chinese email, or Chinese-language follow-up
- the contact already understands the high-level problem and now wants a more concrete overview

## Outreach Sequence

### Sequence A: Cold outbound

1. First touch: send `compare page`
2. No reply after 2 to 3 business days: short follow-up
3. If they reply with interest: send the PDF
4. If they want specifics: offer a `20-minute workflow review`

### Sequence B: Warm intro

1. Intro message: short problem-first explanation
2. First direct note: send `compare page`
3. If they reply positively: send the PDF immediately
4. Close toward a live workflow review

### Sequence C: After a call

1. Send the PDF that matches language
2. Re-state their pain in one sentence
3. Offer one narrow next step:
   - workflow review
   - pilot scope discussion
   - case walkthrough

## Operator Rules

- first touch always uses `compare` or landing, not the PDF
- PDF is for reply, follow-up, or post-call reinforcement
- do not send guest demo first unless they explicitly ask to try the product
- do not send the PDF as a giant attachment blast to strangers
- if they ask about integrations too early, qualify whether they actually have the exception-control pain first
- if they only care about WMS sync, they may not be the right early customer
